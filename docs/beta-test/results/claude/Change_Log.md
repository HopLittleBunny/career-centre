# Change Log

## Application archetype

Stretch application: manager/lead-level operations & transformation candidate applying to a Director enterprise-transformation mandate; positioned on transferable governance, operating-model and adoption evidence.

## Formatting source

Career Centre smart default

## Evidence included
- EV-EXP-YEARS, EV-NS-ONBOARD, EV-NS-GOV, EV-NS-CRM, EV-NS-PARTNER (Northstar)
- EV-HB-JOURNEY, EV-HB-MULTI, EV-HB-DASH, EV-HB-WORKSHOP (Harbour Services)
- EV-BA-REQ, EV-BA-DELIVERY (Eastpoint)
- EV-EDU (Education)

## Evidence excluded
- None - all captured CV claims were usable and included.

## Keywords deliberately included
- operating-model / operating rhythm
- program governance
- decision rights / escalation
- executive stakeholder alignment
- multi-country delivery
- workforce-technology adoption
- change adoption / enablement
- dashboards / delivery risk

## Keywords omitted for evidence safety
- workforce planning - no evidence in CV
- generative AI / AI advisory - no evidence; referenced only as a proposed approach, not a claim
- 'Director' / enterprise-scale P&L or org leadership - not evidenced at that scope
- design assurance / adoption networks - only adjacent enablement evidence, not claimed verbatim

## Ambiguities
- Seniority gap: strongest evidence is manager/lead scope, not Director of enterprise transformation.
- 'Permanent' and salary band taken from the synthetic fixture, not independently verified.
- Employment continuity, team size and budget scope are not stated in the CV and were not invented.
