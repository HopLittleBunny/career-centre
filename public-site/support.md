# Support

Career Centre is published by Amit Sharma.

## Before reporting an issue

Include:

- The workflow that failed: onboarding, search, role assessment, document pack, tracking or interview preparation.
- What you expected and what happened.
- Your ChatGPT surface and approximate date.
- A redacted screenshot or validation report if useful.

Do not post a CV, home address, phone number, email address, work-right document, private employer material or other personal data in a public support request.

## Public support route

Use the [Career Centre GitHub issue form](https://github.com/HopLittleBunny/career-centre/issues/new/choose).

The issue form is public and publisher-controlled. It avoids ongoing infrastructure cost and tells users not to include CV or other personal data.
