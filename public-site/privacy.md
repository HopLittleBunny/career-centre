# Privacy

Effective date: 14 July 2026

Career Centre is a skills-only ChatGPT plugin published by Amit Sharma. It does not use an Amit-operated server, account system, analytics service, advertising service or CV database.

## Information used

The plugin can work with information a user chooses to provide in ChatGPT, including a CV, career preferences, role links, application updates and interview context. It can also use public job-posting information found through ChatGPT's available browsing or search capabilities.

## Where processing happens

The user's ChatGPT environment processes the conversation, attachments, searches and generated files under OpenAI's applicable terms, privacy policy, plan settings and retention controls. Career Centre does not independently transmit that career data to Amit Sharma or to an Amit-operated backend.

Following a job link or using a third-party site is subject to that site's own privacy practices. The plugin does not automatically submit forms or send applications.

## Files and continuity

Career Passport, role dossier and application-pack files may be created inside the user's ChatGPT task or connected workspace when the host supports file creation. The user controls where exported files are saved and shared. Users should avoid including sensitive identifiers that are not needed for career support.

## Retention and deletion

Because there is no publisher-operated account or database, there is no separate Career Centre account to delete. Users manage conversation, attachment and file retention through their ChatGPT account and device controls.

## Contact

Use the public support route listed on the [Support](support.md) page. The final hosted support URL must identify Amit Sharma consistently with the verified OpenAI developer identity.

This page must be reviewed whenever the plugin adds a server, authentication, analytics or another data recipient.
