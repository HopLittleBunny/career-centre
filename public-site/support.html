Support without sharing your CV.

Career Centre is published by Amit Sharma. Public support must stay free of personal career data.

Before reporting an issue

Include the workflow that failed, what you expected, what happened, the ChatGPT surface you used and the approximate date. A redacted screenshot or validation report can help.

Do not post personal data. Remove CV content, home address, phone number, email, work-right documents, private employer material and account details from a public report.

Public support route

Use the public GitHub issue form. It asks for only the product details needed to reproduce a problem.

Useful self-checks

  • Try ChatGPT web if the Skills page is missing elsewhere.
  • Confirm the role link is an exact, open posting.
  • Ask the skill to return its evidence-safety check and document QA summary.
  • For a schedule, confirm the receipt says whether continuity is snapshot-backed or verified persistent.
Never include a CV in a public support report.
