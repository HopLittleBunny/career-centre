# Privacy

This is a skills-only plugin. It does not include an Amit-operated API, database, analytics service or authentication system.

Accurate user-facing wording:

> Career Centre does not send your CV or career history to a publisher-operated backend. Your Claude service still processes the files and conversation under its own terms and workspace controls.

Do not claim that information never leaves the user's device. Do not place secrets, account passwords, government identifiers, referee private details or unnecessary sensitive personal data into the Career Passport.

Only use external web information needed to assess the role. Do not search for private personal information about the candidate.
